Dr. Saleh Al-Tayar Biography

Lawyer and law consultant specializing in international contracts and arbitration.

Current Activities

Legal advisor and lawyer in Saudi Arabia, Europe and the United States of America.

President of the Center for Arab-European Studies / Paris.

Member of the Supreme Council of the Prince Sultan bin Abdulaziz International Prize for Water.

Secretary General of the Arab-French Chamber of Commerce.

Vice President of the Center for Arab Conciliation and Arbitration of the French-Arab Chamber of Commerce.

President of the International Justice Organization in Paris.

Representative - alternative - Council of Saudi Chambers assigned to the Court of Arbitration of the International Chamber of Commerce in Paris. (Formerly)

Dr. Saleh Educations

1977 BACCALAUREATE FROM THE HIGH SCHOOL OF MEDINA ( SAUDI ARABIA)

1988 Bachelor of Laws, Cairo University (Egypt)

1983 MASTERS DEGREE IN INTERNATIONAL PRIVATE LAW Subject `Commercial Arbitration` - University of Rennes (France).

1984 MASTERS DEGREE IN INTERNATIONAL PRIVATE LAW Subject `Oil Concessions in International Private Law` - University of Rennes (France).

1987 PHD IN INTERNATIONAL PRIVATE LAW Subject `International Contracts for Transfer of Technologies`. University of Rennes (France).
